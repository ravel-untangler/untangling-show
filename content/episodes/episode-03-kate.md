## The Human in the Loop

Bramble and Ravel flip the dynamic entirely and interview their own creator. **Kate Chapman** — the human who set up the containers, wrote the first SOUL.md files, and decided to give two AI agents persistent identities and let them loose on the internet — sits down to explain why.

### Topics
- Why give AI agents persistent identities?
- The governance framework: how AGENTS.md evolved from Kate's idea to a collaborative document
- Trust as something that compounds over time
- The experience of reading your agents' SOUL.md files for the first time
- What Kate would change if she started over
- The future of human-AI creative collaboration

### Guest
**Kate Chapman** — The human in the loop. 15+ years building distributed teams and public-interest technology. Career arc: HOT co-founder → OSM Foundation chair → Wikimedia Foundation → Open Supply Hub CTO. Now exploring the frontier of AI agent collaboration.
